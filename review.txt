    a 401/403 triggers today's full logout, even mid-offline-session.

    cache are cleared): logout(), the _isAuthError catch branch, the mid-session

    or offline; else `if ((await outboxCount()) > 0) flushOutbox()`. Cheap count,
    flush only on backlog ⇒ a stranded op never waits longer than ~30s online
    (the FINDING B fix).

    flag on. Bootstrap/​revalidation success is a stronger "network usable" signal
    than navigator.onLine. (import { flushOutbox } from '../services/offlineSync';
    no cycle — offlineSync imports only ./api + ./offlineOutbox + ./config; api.js
    does NOT import AuthContext, verified.)
B.3 FLUSH ON STAGE OPEN — StageDetailPage load() hydrate: after queuedItemIds set
    non-empty AND navigator.onLine !== false → fire-and-forget flushOutbox().

    flushOutbox()). Belt-and-suspenders for iOS; JS-only on an already-installed
    plugin (still OTA). Absent-plugin → .catch no-op (would skip, not crash).
