WHAT & WHY
----------
Bug 4 of the 2026-05-21 report. The unit page back arrow sent a
management user to /projects/{id}/control?tab=structure (the project
structure tab) instead of to the BUILDING the unit sits inside. They
then had to re-drill building → floor → unit just to reach a
neighbouring unit. The unit-detail API already returns the unit's
building {id,name} and UnitHomePage destructures it, so the back arrow
just needs to use it.
