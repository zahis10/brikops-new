fix(plans): align unit-plans archive UX 1:1 with the general page

Bug 3 shipped a "lighter" archive UX on the per-apartment plans
page (inline collapsible section + direct archive button). That
diverged from the general plans page's UX and had a real bug —
archiving the last active plan made the entire archive UI vanish.

/projects/{pid}/units/{uid}/plans/archive page, an "ארכיון" link in
the unit-plans header, and an "להעביר לארכיון?" confirm modal with
an optional note input in place of the direct-archive button.
Frontend-only — reuses Bug 3's backend endpoints unchanged.
