GOAL
----
1. Open SSO/social login to ALL roles (was PM-only).
2. Enforce mandatory ToS + Privacy consent (Israeli Spam Law) on every
   user-creating flow, capturing terms_accepted_at + consent_ip on user_doc.
3. Backfill terms_accepted_at for existing users (one-time, idempotent).
4. SSO accepts invite_token and consumes the invite atomically (role assigned
   from invite, phone-match enforced, membership upserted, status flipped).
