feat(handover): offline photos — meters + defect photos via blob queue (batch 4c)

Final step of the offline arc. Meter photos queue as compressed blobs in a new
handover_meter_photos store (DB_VERSION 5, latest-wins per meter) and drain
AFTER the form drain — upload_meter_photo $sets only the photo_url subfield,
so photos-last guarantees a queued meters-form snapshot can never clobber a
fresh photo. Handover-defect photos ride the 4b item records (photos[] per
record): the drain replays updateItem (idempotent defect reuse), uploads the
blobs to the returned defect_id with incremental de-queue (a partial failure
resumes from the remaining photos — no duplicates), and removes the record
only on full success. Defective-with-photos offline is now allowed
(photos_pending_count satisfies the server rule); zero-photos defective still
requires a skip reason. Tenant ID-photo remains online-gated forever (PII).
Frontend-only; rides OFFLINE_MODE; Capgo OTA.
