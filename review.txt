WHAT & WHY
----------
The per-org storage quota (Phase 2) works, but it has no UI: usage can
only be seen in the org_storage_usage collection in Atlas, and an org's
limit can only be changed by hand-editing that doc or by moving the
global ORG_STORAGE_LIMIT_GB env var (which moves EVERY org at once).

This batch makes it operable from the admin panel:
  - New "אחסון" tab — every org's usage with a fill bar.
  - "שנה מגבלה" sets a per-org limit (limit_bytes on org_storage_usage)
    without touching Atlas.
  - "החזר לברירת מחדל" removes the override — pinning is not a one-way
    door.

The global default (ORG_STORAGE_LIMIT_GB, 100GB) stays as the fallback;
the per-org override lives as `limit_bytes` on the existing
org_storage_usage doc. No new collection, no migration. Revision 2026-05-25b
folds in refactor-backlog #21 (2-query / no N+1 list) and #22 (reset
endpoint).
