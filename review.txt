TWO BUGS FIXED IN THIS BATCH
================================================================================

BUG A — Mixed dropdown is confusing
  Today's "קבלן" dropdown in mgmt panel mixed in one bag: לא שויך,
  specific contractors, company-only "🏢 X — כל החברה" options
  (added in earlier P1 batch), legacy contact_name fallbacks.
  Semantic mismatch — labeled "קבלן" but emits company assignments.
  Fix: split into two dropdowns matching NewDefectModal.js:
    1. "חברה" — picks project_company → sets task.company_id.
    2. "קבלן ספציפי" — filtered by selected company → sets
       task.assignee_id. Disabled / placeholder when no company picked.

BUG B — Display shows generic "קבלן משויך"
  Backend returned task.assignee_name='' for users with empty
  user_name AND empty users.name (legacy / phone-only contractors),
  causing frontend to fall through to "קבלן משויך" in mgmt panel
  AND in the שיוך card above it.
  Fix: extend backend fallback chain → name || phone || email.
  Phone is most identifying for Israeli contractors.
