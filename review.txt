WHAT SHIPPED
------------
Multi-role users (PM in some projects, contractor in others) clicking
a contractor WhatsApp digest link were redirected to the PM dashboard
(no defects shown). Root cause: after Batch 6B v2, `App.js ProjectsHome`
checks the global `user.role` field, which is the highest-privilege
role across all the user's projects. The contractor digest sender
(`reminder_service.send_contractor_reminder`) appends `?src=wa` to its
button URL, so we treat that query param as an explicit
contractor-route signal: arriving from `?src=wa` always shows the
ContractorDashboard regardless of global role.

Future: proper per-project membership lookup is deferred (same family
as Task #32 scope-aware paywall) — `?src=wa` is a reliable signal
because contractor digest is currently the ONLY sender of WA links
to `/projects/:id`.
